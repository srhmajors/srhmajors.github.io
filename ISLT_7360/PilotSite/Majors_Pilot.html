Welcome to Mrs.Majors 8th grade math class!

Hello! My name is Mrs. Majors and I am the 8th grade math teacher for the Jaguars. I look forward to learning with you this year as we explore the 8th grade math curriculum.

Everyday you will need to bring these supplies to class:

  • Spiral notebook
  • Pencil
  • Laptop
  • Calculator (optional)
  • Expo marker (optional)
  • Colored pencils (optional)

8th grade math will prepare you for Algebra 1A in high school. It is a much more rigorous curriculum than 7th grade. You will have homework almost every night. We will spend the first few days getting to know each other and building a community of math learners. It is important to me to create a safe learning environment for all students as we will be making a lot of mistakes as we learn. I will reinforce the fact that we learn so much more from our mistakes than we do from our correct answers so I want everyone to feel safe to share.

"We learn wisdom from failure much more than from success. We often discover what will do, by finding out what will not do; and probably he who never made a mistake never made a discovery” ― Samuel Smiles, The Lives of George and Robert Stephenson

We will cover the following topics this year:

  • Unit 1: Number Sense, Expressions and Equations
  • Unit 2: Functions and Linear Relationships
  • Unit 3: Transformations and Transversals
  • Unit 4: Exponential Functions, Pythagrorean Theorem and Volume
