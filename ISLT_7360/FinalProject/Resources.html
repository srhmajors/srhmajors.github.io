Help Understanding the lesson

If you are struggling with the math content you should:

Teacher Support

Additionally, I am available every day before school starting at 7am, every day during life education and after school with an appointment. At home you can send me an email with the problem you are working on and what you have so far and I will respond to you as soon as I can.

The only reason you will fail this year is if you choose to do so and even then I will be working closely with you to help change that mindset. I am here to help you succeed but you must put in the work to do so.
