Math Review

Review for Tests and Quizzes

In order to be fully prepared for the assessment you should:

  • Ensure all of your homework for the unit is completed and turned in
  • Review your notes in OneNote
  • Review the foldables for the unit
  • Review and correct any homework problems you missed or did not understand
  • Complete the review homework
  • Study with a classmate
  • Ask Mrs. Majors about anything you still do not understand
